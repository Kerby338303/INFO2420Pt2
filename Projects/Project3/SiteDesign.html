Rent A Pet Site Color Palette

This page records the main colors used for the Rent A Pet site. They were selected to work well with the pet images and to provide good contrast for readability.

  • Main background: #FDFAF5
  • Primary text: #222222
  • Accent blue (headings): #2A5D84
  • Accent green (subheadings): #3A7A3F
  • Accent orange (highlights): #B35A1F
  • Link color: #C03A2B
  • Visited link color: #7B2D2D
